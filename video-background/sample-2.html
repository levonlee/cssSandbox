Video as Background

Sample 2

  • video shows 100% across all browsers. Width is 100% and height is auto adjusted according to ratio
  • section is position:relative
  • Child items .lili-div-item position:relative and content within in it is position:absolute
  • .lili-div-item all have 0 height because its child is position:absolute
  • .lili-div-item is used to define width:90% of section
  • Position elements inside .lili-div-item using its own element.
    • .lili-div-item.lili-div-logo img
    • .lili-div-item.lili-div-content-div .lili-div-content
  • Items in .lili-div-item have z-index:100
  • Child item .lili-div-video is also position:relative. video inside it is position:static

iOS v10+ supports autoplay and muted for video tags in non-fullscreen mode while Android supports it long time ago

For iOS <10, create a background image.

poster is supported since iOS 8 and it means to show an image before the frame of video is loaded.
