  • Child items that are placed relative to parent section. Direct child (wrapper) of .lili-div-item are positioned using margin-top: x%;
    • .lili-div-item.lili-div-logo img
    • .lili-div-item.lili-div-content-div .lili-div-content
  • Extra Child Item

Browser Compatibility

  • iOS 10 and up supports autoplay and muted for video tags in non-fullscreen mode
  • Android 4.4 and up support autoplay
  • Windows Phone doesn't support autoplay
  • Create a background image for those devices don't support autoplay.
  • poster is supported since iOS 8 and it means to show an image before the frame of video is loaded.
    • poster in iOS 8 and up can take full width of parent. It's not responsive in iOS 7 and below
    • poster in Windows Phone 8.1 can take full width of parent
    • poster in Android 5 and up is responsive
    • poster in Android 4.4 is not responsive but background-image in video can display underneath
