Scale SVG

CSS Tricks

Cross browser compatible

  • IE and iOS 7 and lower does not scale svg according to viewbox
  • preserveAspectRatio="xMidYMid meet" is default
    • Mid means center, Min or Max is to align to one side
    • meet is equivalent to background-size:contain;
    • slice is equivalent to background-size:cover;
  • 66.67% = svg:height/svg:width
