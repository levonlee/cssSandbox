Caveat

  • From numeric number to percentage might not work

    • Say from top:2800px to top:50% for .child inside body when .child is zoom:40%
    • Have to convert 2800px to percentage value first
    • Say without zoom on child, top:50% has a calculated value of window.getComputedStyle(document.getElementById('child'),null).getPropertyValue('top').match(/\d+/) of (926+35)x0.5 where 926 and 35 are the height and padding-bottom of body
    • Then with zoom:40%, .child has top = (943+35)/0.4x0.5 where 943 and 35 are the height and padding-bottom of body
    • Therefore, top:2800px is equivalent to (body.height + body.padding-bottom)/0.4x%, % is 2800/(body.height + body.padding-bottom)x0.4x100%
    • body.height = parseInt(window.getComputedStyle(document.body,null).height.match(/\d+/)[0])
    • body.padding-bottom = parseInt(window.getComputedStyle(document.body,null).paddingBottom.match(/\d+/)[0])
